News about TODO's team

Dream wedding

Dream wedding. Who doesn't want to hear that? Who doesn't want to have that?

We are proud to say we helped Mihai & Mirela make their dream come true. They had an amazing time in our favourite location - MPalace - in Bucharest, with their family and friends.

You can find more about it here, in the interview taken by our lead - Geta Ungureanu.

"Our team is stronger than ever!"

This is what we heard on a sunnny Monday morning. Rick, the manager of Emag, just got back with feedback about the teambuilding we organized for his team this weekend.

Not only we were very pleased to hear it, but Emag has been working with TODO events for a very long time, so keeping our customer happy and loyal is one of our top priorities

The team, consisting of 20 people, had a nice 4 days trip to Poiana Brasov, a mountain location in Romania. They stayed at Mariotte and had all meals included, and an extra special dinner

We organized different activities, including personalized trainings but also fun ones such as:

  1. Ski and snowboard
  2. Swimming and jacuzzi
  3. Pasta cooking class
  4. Board games night

Overall, everybody was very pleased, or as we say in Romania: Chef pe cinste. See you soon Rick!
